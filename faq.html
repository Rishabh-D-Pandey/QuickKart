Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I place an order?

Placing an order on QuickKart is easy! Browse our products, add items to your cart, and click on the cart icon to proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide your shipping address and payment information. Once confirmed, you'll receive an order confirmation email.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay), UPI payments, net banking, and digital wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay). We also offer cash on delivery (COD) for orders up to ₹10,000.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by location. Major cities: 1-2 days, Tier 2 cities: 2-3 days, Remote areas: 5-7 days. You'll receive tracking information via SMS and email once your order is shipped.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free shipping on all orders above ₹499. For orders below ₹499, a nominal shipping fee of ₹49 will be charged.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the item in its original condition within 30 days for a full refund. Certain items like innerwear, earrings, and opened cosmetics cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect your returned item. The refund will be credited to your original payment method. For COD orders, refunds are credited to your bank account via NEFT.

Do I need to create an account to order?

No, you can checkout as a guest. However, creating an account gives you access to order history, saved addresses, faster checkout, and exclusive offers.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your personal and payment information. Your data is never shared with third parties.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive an SMS and email with your tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section.

Still have questions?

Our support team is here to help you 24/7
